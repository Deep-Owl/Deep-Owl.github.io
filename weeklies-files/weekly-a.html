This week in Art 3001 I learned the fundamentals of HTML.
So far my resources are limited, but I am able to create a basic web page.
I began working on the materials outside of class by researching some artists who also use web art as a primary medium. 
Some of these artists came up as examples in class.

My favorite artists out of the ones I found were:
  • Olufar Eliasson
  • Rafael Rozendaal
  • Casey Reas

After doing some light research and bookmarking the HTML reference provided by Mozilla, I began writing some HTML.
I was having a hard time figuring out what I wanted my first project to look and feel like.
In order to alleviate my creative block I just started writing a stream of conciousness using the preformatted tag.
I then tried to space out each line of text so that it would be very nicely formatted.
I also italicized every word that I made up just for fun.
After that I attempted to make a simple design by using the horizontal line tag.
